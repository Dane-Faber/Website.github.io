BMW Facts

BMW Motorrad has a long history of producing motorcycles, since 1923, with notable features such as the horizontally opposed twin-cylinder engine, which provides a low center of gravity, and a focus on touring and adventure riding. They have been pioneers in motorcycle technology with the introduction of anti-lock brakes (ABS) and electronic suspension systems. In recent years, BMW has also embraced electric motorcycles. BMW motorcycles are known for their quality engineering, innovative features, and iconic design elements, making them popular among riders who value comfort, performance, and adventure.
